# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, or nationality.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Use welcoming language.
- Be mindful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Offensive comments or derogatory language.
- Personal attacks or threats.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainers directly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, block users, or take other actions to maintain a safe community. Serious or repeated violations may result in exclusion from the project.
